Beach Lover App

Beach Lover is an app designed for beach goers to plan their most excellent beach experience.

Beach Lover geolocates users, reveals nearby beaches and displays beach features alongside today's hourly weather at each location.

Frameworks:

  • Javascript, jQuery, Angular.js, Twitter Bootstrap
  • HTML 5 Geolocation
  • Leaflet.js
  • Marine Weather API
